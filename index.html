Meet the Speakers

Prof. Neena Gupta

Professor, Statistics & mathematical unit Indian Statistical Institute(ISI), Kolkata

Topic: Fermat's descent principle

She is known for solving Zariski Cancellation Problem, originally posed by Oscar Zariski in 1949. Her primary fields of interest are commutative algebra and affine algebraic geometry.

About Symmetry '25

Symmetry '25 is the premier annual mathematics festival that celebrates the beauty and elegance of mathematical thinking. This year's event brings together students, researchers, and enthusiasts from across the country to explore the fascinating world of mathematics through interactive workshops, competitions, and presentations.

Our festival combines classical mathematical traditions with modern computational approaches, reflecting the perfect symmetry between historical wisdom and contemporary innovation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in Symmetry '25?

Students from any college or university can participate in the festival. Some events may have specific eligibility criteria which will be mentioned in the event details.

Is there any registration fee?

Yes, registration fee is â‚¹120 for all participants which covers for all the events and lunch.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can register for multiple events. However, please check the event schedules to avoid conflicts. In particular you can either participate in the Sudoku or in the Paper Presentation. In case of registration to both the events you will be selected only for one event based on the availability of seats in the events.

What should I bring for the event?

Please bring your college ID, writing materials, and any specific equipment mentioned in the event guidelines. Laptops may be required for coding events.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes, participation certificates will be provided to all participants, and winners will receive special recognition certificates and prizes.
